01 / 研究方向01 / RESEARCH

用进化视角理解
肿瘤治疗Understanding therapy
through evolution

围绕胃癌耐药这一临床瓶颈，我关注肿瘤在治疗压力下如何改变、适应与锁定演化路径。

To address drug resistance in gastric cancer, I study how tumors change, adapt and lock in evolutionary paths under therapeutic pressure.

01

ecDNA 与耐药演化ecDNA & drug resistance

解析染色体外 DNA 如何加速胃癌获得性耐药，并探索靶向 DNA-PK 的联合治疗策略。

Investigating how extrachromosomal DNA accelerates acquired resistance and exploring combination therapy strategies targeting DNA-PK.

02

天然分子与小分子药物Natural & small molecules

基于肿瘤进化模型开展天然产物转化研究，并通过分子与细胞功能学寻找干预机制。

Translating natural products through tumor-evolution models and uncovering intervention mechanisms with molecular and cellular studies.

03

药物网络与联合治疗Drug networks & combinations

从“药物—靶点”网络出发，分析胃癌联合治疗的配伍优势及临床转化潜力。

Mapping drug-target networks to reveal combination advantages and translational opportunities in gastric cancer therapy.

研究模型 / PATH-DEPENDENT EVOLUTIONRESEARCH MODEL / PATH-DEPENDENT EVOLUTION

治疗压力下的路径选择Path selection under therapeutic pressure

我们关注 ecDNA 事件如何触发适应临界，使肿瘤从低适应路径进入扩增路径，或形成耐药复发路径的“锁定”。

We study how ecDNA events may trigger an adaptive threshold, moving tumors from low-adaptation routes toward amplification or path-locked resistance and relapse.
